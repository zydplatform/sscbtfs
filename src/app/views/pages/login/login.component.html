FacilitatingTradeAcross Borders

Small-scale cross-border trade (SSCBT) is of substantial importance in African countries. The existence of marked price differences due to variations in production capacities, trade barriers, and regulatory compliance costs, combined with the absence of geographical or social separators, motivate many individuals to trade small consignments across borders.
